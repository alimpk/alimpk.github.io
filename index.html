Ali Mo. is a master research assistant at computer engineering department of Amirkbir Universiry of Technology (Tehran Polytechnic).His research focuses on efficient deep learning computing and is also interested in reconfigurable systems and the RISC-V computer architecture.
He has been involved in several open source and commercial projects related to acceleration and implementation of computer vision, signal processing, and machine learning systems on the field-programmable gate arrays. Hi has participated in several companies as a senior computer hardware engineer or hardware and software co-design (hardware and software interface design).
